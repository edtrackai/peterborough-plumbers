# Content Skills — Peterborough Plumbers

## Core Rules

- Sound natural and human — never generic AI content
- Professional but approachable UK English tone
- Conversion-focused with CTAs on every page
- Local audience first (Peterborough and surrounding areas)
- Every piece of content serves an SEO purpose

---

## Heading Structure — Strict Hierarchy (MANDATORY)

- EXACTLY ONE `<h1>` per page — no exceptions
- H1 MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough" (or area name)
- H1 MUST be the first heading on the page
- Headings follow logical order: H1 → H2 → H3 → H4 (never skip levels)
- MINIMUM 3 H2s per page, each targeting different keyword variations
- NEVER use headings for visual styling — use CSS classes

**H1 Formulas:**
```
Homepage:  "Trusted Plumbers in Peterborough — [USP]"
Service:   "[Service Name] in Peterborough — [Modifier]"
Area:      "Plumber in [Area Name] [Postcode] — [Modifier]"
Blog:      "[Question/How-To] — Expert Advice"
```

**H2 Examples — Boiler Service Page:**
- "What's Included in Our Boiler Service?"
- "Boiler Service Cost in Peterborough"
- "Why Annual Boiler Servicing Matters"
- "Gas Safe Boiler Engineers Near You"
- "Frequently Asked Questions"
- "Book Your Boiler Service Today"

## Keyword Integration Rules

- Primary keyword: 1-2% density (natural, never forced)
- Location keyword ("Peterborough"): 3-5 mentions per page minimum
- Long-tail variations: 2-3 unique per page
- LSI/related terms: use synonyms naturally (plumber/plumbing/pipework/heating engineer)
- NEVER keyword stuff — content must read naturally to humans

---

## Meta Tag Content Rules

### Title Tags (50-60 characters)
- MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough" + power modifier
- Power modifiers: Trusted, Professional, Emergency, Certified, Same-Day, 24/7, Affordable, Licensed, Expert, Gas Safe
- NEVER duplicate titles across pages
```
✅ "Boiler Service Peterborough | Gas Safe Engineers — From £79"
✅ "Plumber in Werrington PE4 | Same-Day — Peterborough Plumbers"
❌ "Welcome to Our Website" (no keyword, no location)
❌ "Peterborough Plumbers Peterborough Plumbing" (stuffed)
```

### Meta Descriptions (140-160 characters)
- MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough"
- MUST contain CTA (Call now, Book today, Get a free quote)
- MUST include trust signal (Gas Safe, 30+ years, 4.6★)
- MUST be unique per page — NEVER leave empty
```
✅ "Gas Safe registered plumbers in Peterborough with 30+ years experience. Boiler service, emergency repairs, bathrooms. Call 01234 567890 for a free quote."
✅ "Annual boiler service from £79 by Gas Safe engineers in Peterborough. Keep your heating safe & efficient. Book online or call today."
```

## H1 Uniqueness Rule — MANDATORY

- No two pages on the site may share the same H1 — ever
- Each H1 must be unique enough that swapping it between two pages would be obviously wrong
- Before finalising any H1, check it against other pages in the same category

```
❌ Two service pages both using "Expert Plumbers in Peterborough"
✅ "Boiler Service in Peterborough — Gas Safe Engineers"
✅ "Emergency Plumber in Peterborough — Available 24/7"
```

---

## Duplicate Content Prevention — Area Pages

Area pages are high-risk for duplicate content because they cover similar services in similar locations.
Each area page MUST have unique content that could not appear on any other page:

- Unique area description mentioning real local landmarks, streets, or neighbourhoods
- Unique response time claim specific to that area
- Unique local customer testimonial (from a real customer in that area)
- Unique FAQ questions framed for that specific area
- Unique intro paragraph — not a find-and-replace of another area page

**Test:** Copy-paste two area pages into a diff tool. If they are >60% identical by word, rewrite.

## Anchor Text — Forbidden List

NEVER use these as anchor text anywhere on the site:

```
❌ "click here"
❌ "learn more"
❌ "read more"
❌ "this page"
❌ "here"
❌ "link"
❌ "visit us"
❌ "find out more"
```

ALL anchor text must describe the destination page content using keywords:
```
✅ "our boiler service in Peterborough"
✅ "emergency plumber available 24/7"
✅ "gas safety certificates for landlords"
✅ "plumber in Werrington PE4"
```

---

## Reading Level & Tone

- Target UK Flesch reading ease score: **60–70** (plain English, accessible to all adults)
- Sentence length: maximum 25 words per sentence on average
- Paragraph length: maximum 4 sentences
- Use contractions naturally: "we're", "you'll", "don't" — sounds human
- Avoid jargon without explanation: if you say "CP12", explain it's a gas safety certificate
- Never use American spelling: "colour" not "color", "recognised" not "recognized", "whilst" not "while"
- Avoid filler phrases: "in today's world", "without further ado", "it's important to note"

---

## Internal Link Depth — MANDATORY

Every page on the site must be reachable within 3 clicks from the homepage:
- Homepage → Services index → Service page (2 clicks) ✅
- Homepage → Areas index → Area page (2 clicks) ✅
- Homepage → Blog index → Blog post (2 clicks) ✅

No orphan pages — every page must have at least one internal link pointing to it.
