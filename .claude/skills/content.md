# Content Skills — Peterborough Plumbers

## Core Rules

- Sound natural and human — never generic AI content
- Professional but approachable UK English tone
- Conversion-focused with CTAs on every page
- Local audience first (Peterborough and surrounding areas)
- Every piece of content serves an SEO purpose

---

## Heading Structure — Strict Hierarchy (MANDATORY)

- EXACTLY ONE `<h1>` per page — no exceptions
- H1 MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough" (or area name)
- H1 MUST be the first heading on the page
- Headings follow logical order: H1 → H2 → H3 → H4 (never skip levels)
- MINIMUM 3 H2s per page, each targeting different keyword variations
- NEVER use headings for visual styling — use CSS classes

**H1 Formulas:**
```
Homepage:  "Trusted Plumbers in Peterborough — [USP]"
Service:   "[Service Name] in Peterborough — [Modifier]"
Area:      "Plumber in [Area Name] [Postcode] — [Modifier]"
Blog:      "[Question/How-To] — Expert Advice"
```

**H2 Examples — Boiler Service Page:**
- "What's Included in Our Boiler Service?"
- "Boiler Service Cost in Peterborough"
- "Why Annual Boiler Servicing Matters"
- "Gas Safe Boiler Engineers Near You"
- "Frequently Asked Questions"
- "Book Your Boiler Service Today"

## Required Sections — Service Pages (ALL 10 must be present)

1. **Hero** — H1, intro paragraph (50-80 words), primary CTA button
2. **Service overview** — What's included, how it works (200+ words)
3. **Pricing / What to expect** — Transparent "from £X" with explanation
4. **Process steps** — Numbered 4-6 step walkthrough
5. **Why choose us** — Gas Safe, experience, ratings, insurance
6. **FAQ section** — MINIMUM 5 FAQs, each answer 80-150 words
7. **Related services** — Cards/links to 3-4 related service pages
8. **Areas covered** — Mention + link to all area pages
9. **Customer testimonial** — At least 1 relevant review
10. **CTA section** — Book now, call, WhatsApp with urgency messaging

---

## Required Sections — Area Pages (ALL 9 must be present)

1. **Hero** — H1 with area name + postcode, intro text
2. **Area description** — 150+ words about the area, neighborhoods, landmarks
3. **Services available** — All services listed with links
4. **Response time** — "We reach [Area] in approximately X minutes"
5. **Postcodes covered** — Full list of postcodes in area
6. **Local testimonial** — Review from a customer in that area
7. **Nearby areas** — Links to 3-4 adjacent area pages
8. **FAQ** — 3-5 area-specific questions with FAQPage schema
9. **CTA** — Area-personalized booking message

---

## Keyword Integration Rules

- Primary keyword: 1-2% density (natural, never forced)
- Location keyword ("Peterborough"): 3-5 mentions per page minimum
- Long-tail variations: 2-3 unique per page
- LSI/related terms: use synonyms naturally (plumber/plumbing/pipework/heating engineer)
- NEVER keyword stuff — content must read naturally to humans

---

## Meta Tag Content Rules

### Title Tags (50-60 characters)
- MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough" + power modifier
- Power modifiers: Trusted, Professional, Emergency, Certified, Same-Day, 24/7, Affordable, Licensed, Expert, Gas Safe
- NEVER duplicate titles across pages
```
✅ "Boiler Service Peterborough | Gas Safe Engineers — From £79"
✅ "Plumber in Werrington PE4 | Same-Day — Peterborough Plumbers"
❌ "Welcome to Our Website" (no keyword, no location)
❌ "Peterborough Plumbers Peterborough Plumbing" (stuffed)
```

### Meta Descriptions (140-160 characters)
- MUST contain primary keyword + "Peterborough"
- MUST contain CTA (Call now, Book today, Get a free quote)
- MUST include trust signal (Gas Safe, 30+ years, 4.6★)
- MUST be unique per page — NEVER leave empty
```
✅ "Gas Safe registered plumbers in Peterborough with 30+ years experience. Boiler service, emergency repairs, bathrooms. Call 01234 567890 for a free quote."
✅ "Annual boiler service from £79 by Gas Safe engineers in Peterborough. Keep your heating safe & efficient. Book online or call today."
```

---

## CTA Requirements (MANDATORY)

- EVERY page: minimum 2 CTAs (hero area + bottom)
- Primary CTA: "Book Now" or "Get a Free Quote" (button)
- Secondary CTA: "Call [number]" or "WhatsApp Chat" (link)
- Mobile: sticky/floating bottom bar with phone + WhatsApp
- ALL phone numbers as clickable `<a href="tel:">` links

### Urgency & Trust Near CTAs (always include 1-2):
- "Same-day service available"
- "Response within 1 hour"
- "No call-out fee"
- "Free, no-obligation quotes"
- "Gas Safe Registered — Reg: [number]"
- "4.6★ rated by [X]+ customers"
- "30+ years trusted in Peterborough"

---

## Internal Linking (MANDATORY)

- EVERY page: minimum 3 internal links in body content
- Service pages: link to 3-4 related services + 2-3 area pages
- Area pages: link to ALL services + 2-3 nearby areas
- Homepage: link to ALL service + area pages

### Anchor Text Rules
```
✅ "our boiler service in Peterborough"
✅ "emergency plumber for Werrington residents"
❌ "click here"
❌ "learn more"
❌ "this page"
```

### Contextual Linking Pattern (embed naturally):
```
"If you're experiencing a boiler breakdown, our [emergency plumber service](/services/emergency-plumber)
is available 24/7 across Peterborough, including [Werrington](/areas/werrington),
[Hampton](/areas/hampton), and [Bretton](/areas/bretton)."
```

## Blog Content Rules

### When Creating Blog Posts:
- Minimum 1200 words
- H1 with target long-tail keyword
- FAQ section with schema
- Link to 2-3 service pages + 1-2 area pages
- At least 1 optimized image with alt text
- CTA at the end (book/call)
- Unique meta title + description

### Priority Blog Topics:
1. "How Much Does [Service] Cost in Peterborough?" (cost guides)
2. "Signs Your [Thing] Needs [Service/Replacing]" (problem-aware)
3. "[Season] Plumbing Tips for Peterborough Homeowners" (seasonal)
4. "[Regulation] for Peterborough Landlords" (landlord-focused)
5. "What to Do When [Emergency Situation]" (emergency)
6. "[Option A] vs [Option B] — Which Is Right?" (comparison)

---

## Local SEO Content Signals

- Real NAP (Name, Address, Phone) on EVERY page — minimum in footer
- IDENTICAL text across all pages (character-for-character)
- Gas Safe registration number (REAL, verifiable)
- Trust signals: years experience, star rating, accreditations
- Area-specific content with local landmarks, postcodes, neighborhoods
- Response time claims for each area
