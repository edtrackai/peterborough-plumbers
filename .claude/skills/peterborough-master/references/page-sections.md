# Page Sections & Content Structure

Mandatory section requirements for each page type. All content in UK English. Include blog topic priorities at end.

---

## SERVICE PAGES (10 Sections Minimum)

Each service page must have these sections in order:

### 1. Hero Section
- H1 title: "[Service Name] in Peterborough"
- Large background image (service-related)
- 60-80 word intro
- Primary CTA button: "Book Now" or "Get Quote"
- Secondary CTA: "Call [PHONE]"

### 2. Service Overview
- 100-150 words explaining what the service is
- Who needs this service
- Key benefits (3-4 bullet points)
- Image: service in action

### 3. Pricing Section
- Clear pricing tiers or starting price
- "Price from: £[X]" format
- What's included
- Factors that affect price
- Link to detailed pricing page
- Transparent note: "Free callout, no obligation"

### 4. Process Steps
- 4-6 numbered steps
- Each step: title + 40-60 words
- Icons/illustrations for each step
- Example: Inspection → Diagnosis → Quote → Approval → Work → Completion
- Emphasise speed/professionalism

### 5. Why Choose Us Section
- 4 key differentiators
- Each with icon, title, 50-word description
- Examples: "Certified Engineers", "24/7 Emergency", "10-Year Guarantee", "Transparent Pricing"
- Build trust and confidence

### 6. FAQ Section
- Minimum 5 questions
- 80-150 words per answer
- Questions targeting intent (cost, time, process, safety, etc.)
- Use <details> element for accessibility
- Schema: FAQPage in JSON-LD

### 7. Related Services Section
- 3-4 card grid
- Thumbnail image + service title + brief description
- Link to each related service page
- Example: If "Boiler Repairs" → show "Boiler Installation", "Central Heating", "Gas Safety Checks"

### 8. Areas Covered Section
- "We serve these areas around Peterborough"
- 6-9 area links in 2-3 column grid
- Area names with postcode ranges
- Link to areas listing page
- "Not listed? Call us for coverage"

### 9. Testimonial Section
- 1 featured testimonial or carousel of 3-5
- Star rating (5 stars ideally)
- Customer name, area, service
- 50-100 word review
- Include profile image if available
- Schema: Review in JSON-LD

### 10. Call-to-Action (CTA) Section
- Final conversion push
- H2: "Ready to fix your [service]?"
- Two buttons: "Book Online" + "Call Now [PHONE]"
- Urgency element: "Same-day appointments available"
- Address phone numbers as clickable tel: links

## BLOG POSTS (7 Sections Minimum)

SEO-focused content to drive organic traffic and build authority.

### 1. Hero with Metadata
- H1 title (50-60 characters, keyword-rich)
- Featured image (1200x630px, WebP)
- Metadata: 
  - Published date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  - Reading time: "X min read"
  - Author: [Business Name]
  - Last updated (if revised)

### 2. Introduction Section
- 80-120 words
- Answer the "why should I read this?" question
- Hook the reader with a relatable problem
- Preview the 3-5 main points
- NO H2 yet (stays under H1)

### 3. Main Body Content (3+ H2 sections)
- **Section 1 (H2)**: 200-300 words, main topic intro
- **Section 2 (H2)**: 250-350 words, deep dive or how-to
- **Section 3 (H2)**: 200-300 words, tips/best practices
- **Optional Section 4-5 (H2)**: Additional insights
- Natural keyword integration (1-2% keyword density)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points and lists for scanability

### 4. Images Section
- Minimum 2-3 relevant images embedded within body
- Placement: Every 200-300 words
- Format: WebP (with JPG fallback)
- Alt text: Descriptive (not keyword-stuffed)
- Caption: Optional, adds context
- Image specs: 800x450px or 1200x675px (Next.js Image optimised)
- Example: boiler-repair-process-peterborough.webp

### 5. FAQ Section
- 3-5 questions extracted from blog content
- These should answer reader questions
- Each answer: 60-100 words
- Use <details> element for accessibility
- Schema: FAQPage in JSON-LD
- Examples:
  - "What is the average cost of [service]?"
  - "How often should I [maintain service]?"
  - "Is [service] covered by warranty?"
  - "What happens if I ignore [problem]?"

### 6. Related Services Section
- "Related Services We Offer"
- 3-4 service cards
- Service name + 40-word description + "Learn More" link
- If blog is about "Boiler Repairs", link to:
  - Boiler Installation
  - Central Heating
  - Emergency Plumbing
  - Gas Safety Checks

### 7. Call-to-Action (CTA) Section
- "Need professional help?"
- 2-3 sentence pitch based on blog topic
- Two buttons: "Book Service" + "Call [PHONE]" (tel: link)
- Example for boiler article:
  - "If you're experiencing boiler issues, don't wait. Our certified engineers are available today."

### 6. Call-to-Action (CTA) Section
- "Ready to experience our service?"
- Two buttons: "Book Now" + "Call [PHONE]" (tel: link)

## BLOG TOPIC PRIORITIES

### TIER 1: High Intent + High Volume (Do First)
Target commercial keywords with clear user intent.

1. **"Boiler Repairs Peterborough"**
   - Cost ranges, common issues, emergency response
   - Target: Emergency service seekers, high conversion

2. **"Boiler Installation Peterborough"**
   - New install process, system types, grants available
   - Target: Homeowners considering replacement

3. **"Leaking Tap/Pipe Repair Peterborough"**
   - DIY warning, water bill impact, quick fixes
   - Target: Homeowners with urgent issues

4. **"Central Heating Not Working Peterborough"**
   - Troubleshooting steps, when to call engineer
   - Target: Frustrated customers needing immediate help

5. **"Gas Safety Inspection Peterborough"**
   - Legal requirement, what's checked, frequency
   - Target: Landlords and tenants (legal angle)

### TIER 2: Medium Intent + Medium Volume (Secondary)
Informational content building topical authority.

6. **"Radiator Bleeding: How-To Guide"**
   - Step-by-step, cost, when needed
   - Target: DIY-inclined homeowners

7. **"Boiler Maintenance Tips"**
   - Annual servicing, cost-saving, longevity
   - Target: Proactive homeowners

8. **"What Does a Gas Safe Engineer Do?"**
   - Qualification overview, safety focus
   - Target: Trust-building, educational

9. **"Drain Cleaning & Unblocking Peterborough"**
   - Signs, costs, prevention
   - Target: Homeowners with slow drains

10. **"Airing Cupboard Heating: Modern Solutions"**
    - Traditional vs. modern, efficiency
    - Target: Older home owners

### TIER 3: Low Intent + Lower Volume (Build Authority)
Seasonal or evergreen authority content.

11. **"Winter Boiler Preparation Checklist"** (Seasonal: Oct-Nov)
12. **"Preventing Frozen Pipes in Peterborough"** (Seasonal: Dec-Feb)
13. **"Underfloor Heating Installation Guide"** (Evergreen)
14. **"Thermostatic Radiator Valves: Efficiency Upgrade"** (Evergreen)
15. **"Commercial Plumbing vs. Residential: What's Different"** (B2B)
16. **"Types of Boilers: Combi vs. System vs. Regular"** (Buying guide)
17. **"How to Read Your Gas Meter"** (Evergreen, educational)
18. **"Signs You Need a New Boiler"** (Upgrade-focused)
19. **"First-Time Buyer's Guide to Home Plumbing"** (New homeowner)
20. **"Water Softener Installation & Benefits"** (Upsell/educational)

### TIER 4: Niche Authority (Quarterly)
Low volume but highly targeted.

21. **"Combating Hard Water in Peterborough"** (Regional issue)
22. **"Loft Insulation & Heating Efficiency"** (Cross-functional)
23. **"Eco-Friendly Plumbing Solutions"** (Sustainability angle)
24. **"The Cost of Ignoring Water Leaks"** (Scare+solve)
25. **"Why Monthly Gas Safety Checks for Rentals"** (Legal/landlord)

### Publication Schedule

- **Months 1-2**: Publish Tier 1 articles (5 posts, 1 per week)
- **Months 3-4**: Publish Tier 2 articles (5 posts, 1 per week)
- **Months 5+**: Mix Tier 3 & 4, plus seasonal updates
- **Ongoing**: Update old Tier 1 articles quarterly for freshness

## CONTENT GUIDELINES FOR ALL PAGES

1. **UK English**: favour, colour, organisation, centre, programme
2. **Phone Links**: Always use `<a href="tel:+441733XXXXXX">[Display Number]</a>`
3. **Postcodes**: Format as "PE1 1AB" (space between letters and numbers)
4. **Measurements**: Use Celsius for temperature, litres for volume, cm for small dimensions
5. **Currency**: £ symbol, e.g., "£99.50"
6. **Tone**: Professional but approachable; helpful, not pushy
7. **CTAs**: Always include at least 2 conversion paths (Book online + Call)
8. **Accessibility**: Alt text on all images, descriptive link text, logical heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
9. **SEO**: 1-2% keyword density, internal linking (3-5 links per page), schema markup on all pages
10. **Mobile-First**: Content scannable on mobile (short paragraphs, bullet points, headings)
